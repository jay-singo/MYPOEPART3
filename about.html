About TechGadgets SA

Established in 2015, TechGadgets SA is South Africa's leading e-commerce platform specializing in premium technology gadgets and smart accessories. We serve customers across all nine provinces with fast, reliable delivery.

Our Story

What started as a passion project in Johannesburg has grown into South Africa's most trusted tech retailer. Our founder, Alex Maseko, identified a gap in the local market for authentic, high-quality tech products at competitive prices.

Today, we're proud partners with global brands like Apple, Samsung, and Xiaomi, bringing the latest innovations directly to South African tech enthusiasts.
